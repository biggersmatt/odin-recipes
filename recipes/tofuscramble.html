Tofu Scramble

Description

This is a recipe for a tofu scramble!

Ingredients
  • 16oz Tofu
  • 2 Tbsps Nutrional Yeast
  • 1 Tbsp Olive Oil
  • 1/2 tsp Garlic Powder
Steps
  • Press the tofu
  • Add olive oil to pan on medium heat
  • Add pressed tofu to pan
  • Add spices to tofu and stir
